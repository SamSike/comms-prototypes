Welcome to UniGadget

UniGadget is a second-hand electronics trading site made for students and staff at the University of Melbourne.

After logging in with your student ID, you can browse used phones, laptops, and more. Filter by category, condition (90% new), or price (e.g., under ¥100).

Click on an item to see full details like original price, seller’s price, battery condition, and warranty. You can favorite items or contact the seller.

Want to sell? Click the big orange button on the homepage. Upload photos and key info to list your device.

There’s also a quick price estimator—just enter the name, model, and answer a few questions. Your profile shows your avatar, nickname, and level.
